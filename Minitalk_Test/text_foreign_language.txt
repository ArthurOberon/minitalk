(“An honest man has nothing to fear, so I’m trying my best not to be afraid.” — Frank Abagnale Jr. Catch Me If You Can)

("They believe whatever interpretation of the truth is most convenient to them. I sell them the illusion of their wildest dreams come true." — Laurent Thierry. Great Pretender)
